Bulgogi

Description

Bulgogi, literally "fire meat", is a gui made of thin, marinated slices of meat, most commonly beef, grilled on a barbecue or on a stove-top griddle. It is also often stir-fried in a pan in home cooking. Sirloin, rib eye or brisket are frequently used cuts of beef for the dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef tenderloin, top sirloin, or skirt steak), slice into ⅛-inch-thick slices
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 green onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds, coarsely ground
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Make marinade
  2. Add beef and mix by hand
  3. Let beef sit covered in marinade in the fridge for at least 30 minutes
  4. Cook on a pan or grill (vegetables can be added in this step)
  5. Serve with rice
